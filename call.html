Join Multiplicity 2021!

We are looking for writers, designers, web developers, and editors to join us for our next issue!

Apply by January 12th 2021 to write a piece here, or to design, edit, or do web work here.

What is Multplicity?

Multiplicity is a digital curation on the internet's place in our lives, on both a personal and socio-political level, as young people in different parts of the world. We critically examine the internet's influence, and explore ways to build a healtheir and more equitable internet. For more on the project's inception and goals, read our origin story. We hope to curate a diverse issue from the backgrounds of the writers to the topics explored.

What content are we looking for?

The majority of the writing will be research-based and investigative, but a few exceptions will be made for other opinion pieces and artistic expressions. We are aiming to host 10-12 articles in the upcoming issue.

Contributors are encouraged, but not required, to incorporate interactive work, data investigations, or ethnographic studies in their work. We have a team of data visualization and interactive design volunteers to support you with any needs!

Articles should be 1500-2500 words each (7-15 minutes of reading).

Who is our target audience?

Writing will be made as accessible as possible to widen potential viewership, and will cover a broad range of disciplines. The level of writing should be accessible to a high school/undergraduate level college student with good English proficiency.
